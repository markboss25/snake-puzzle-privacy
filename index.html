Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game Snake Puzzle: Brain Logic Game published by Alpha Future Studio.

Information We Collect

Snake Puzzle: Brain Logic Game does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, camera, microphone, SMS, or call data.

Local Game Data

The game may store gameplay progress and settings locally on your device, such as unlocked levels, stars, sound settings, music settings, vibration settings, and restart counters. This data is stored only on your device and is not sent to our servers.

Advertising

Snake Puzzle: Brain Logic Game may show advertisements. Advertising may be provided by third-party advertising services, such as Google AdMob or other ad networks. These services may collect or use device information, app interactions, approximate location derived from network data, advertising identifiers, and diagnostic information to provide, limit, measure, and improve ads.

Third-party advertising providers may use cookies, advertising identifiers, or similar technologies according to their own privacy policies. You can manage or reset your device advertising ID and ad personalization settings in your device settings where available.

In-App Purchases

Snake Puzzle: Brain Logic Game may offer optional in-app purchases, such as removing ads or unlocking additional features. Purchases are processed by the app store provider, such as Google Play. We do not receive or store your full payment card details. The app store provider may process payment information according to its own privacy policy and terms.

Device Permissions

Snake Puzzle: Brain Logic Game may use the device vibration feature for gameplay feedback. This permission is used only to trigger vibration effects during gameplay and does not collect personal data.

How We Use Information

Local gameplay data is used only to provide core game functionality, including saving progress, remembering settings, and improving the gameplay experience on the same device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Some data may be shared with third-party service providers that help operate the game, such as advertising networks and app store purchase processors. These third parties process data according to their own privacy policies and are responsible for their own data handling practices.

Data Retention and Deletion

Local game data remains on your device until you delete the app, clear the app data in your device settings, or reset your device. You can delete local data by uninstalling the game or clearing its app storage. Data processed by third-party advertising or purchase services is retained according to those providers' own policies.

Children's Privacy

Snake Puzzle: Brain Logic Game is a casual puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Security

We use reasonable measures to keep local game data limited to gameplay functionality. When data is transmitted by third-party services, such as advertising or purchase providers, those services are expected to use appropriate security measures such as encrypted transmission where required.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
